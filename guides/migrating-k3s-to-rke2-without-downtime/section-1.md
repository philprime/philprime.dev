This section introduces the migration project, explains why you might choose RKE2 over k3s, and guides you through
developing a comprehensive migration strategy. You will learn how to audit your existing infrastructure and plan
for a zero-downtime transition.

## Topics Covered

- Understanding the migration objectives and expected outcomes
- Comparing k3s and RKE2: features, use cases, and trade-offs
- Developing a phased migration strategy
- Conducting an infrastructure audit and preparing prerequisites

By the end of this section, you will have a clear understanding of what the migration entails and a solid plan for
executing it safely.
